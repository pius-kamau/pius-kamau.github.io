About me

Get to know the engineer behind the code.

I'm a junior backend developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I focus on the fundamentals — APIs, databases, authentication, and the boring stuff that makes applications stay online.

I don't chase every new framework. I want to understand how things work under the hood, build solid foundations, and eventually contribute to systems that thousands of people rely on.

Before focusing on backend development, I explored full-stack concepts but realized my passion lies in server-side logic, data modeling, and system architecture.

When I'm not coding, I read about distributed systems, contribute to open-source discussions, and sharpen my understanding of database internals.

My engineering principles

⚡ Simplicity over cleverness — Code that's easy to understand is easier to maintain.
🔒 Security by design — Authentication and authorization aren't afterthoughts.
📊 Data integrity first — Your database should tell the truth, always.
🔄 Idempotent where possible — Safe retries matter in real systems.
